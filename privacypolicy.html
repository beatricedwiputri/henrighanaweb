Privacy Policy

This website is operated by the Health Equity, Religion, & NTDs Research Institute, Ghana. We respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting the personal information submitted through this website.

Information We Collect

The website may collect basic personal information submitted voluntarily through forms on the website. This may include name, email address, phone number, organization information, location, and any messages provided by the user.

How the Information is Used

The information collected is used solely for communication purposes such as responding to inquiries, scheduling meetings, facilitating collaborations, and supporting research-related activities conducted by the institute.

Data Storage and Security

Submitted data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized administrators of the website. We take reasonable measures to ensure that personal information is handled responsibly and protected from unauthorized access.

Sharing of Information

Personal information submitted through this website will not be sold or shared with third parties unless required for operational purposes or permitted by the user.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or wish to request the removal of your submitted information, please contact us at henrig2030@gmail.com.
